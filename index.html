Welcome Home

Home is the best place to be!

Except maybe when you’ve been there too long and you can’t have friends over.

Isolation is not fun!

We are a new social media site aimed at local communities, so home can feel a little broader than just inside our own walls.

We want to help families connect, share solutions for homeschool, play, coping with everyday life.

At the difficult times we want to be the best example for our children that we can be. We are stronger together, in our community.

So join us and connect with other households in your community to support and encourage each other.
